About Me


My name is Laura Saxton, and I'm currently reading for the Msc Cyber Security at the University of Essex. Before undertaking this endeavor, I had the opportunity to live and work around the world in a variety of roles. I conducted behavioural research on elephants in Thailand, worked as an English Program Manager in Cambodia, and as an HIV Counselor in Kenya, just to name a few. For the past several years I have worked as an English Teacher in Bangkok, Thailand.

While these experiences have been wonderful, I am looking for a new challenge. I find privacy and security in the digital age to be of paramount importance, and want to tackle this subject head on to the best of my abilities. I hope to use what I learn on this course to become a Certified Ethical Hacker (C|EH), and eventually a Pentester (OSCP).
